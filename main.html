More About Black Clover

Asta is a young boy who dreams of becoming the greatest mage in
the kingdom. Only one problem—he can’t use any magic! Luckily
for Asta, he receives the incredibly rare five-leaf clover grimoire
that gives him the power of anti-magic. Can someone who can’t use
magic really become the Wizard King? One thing’s for sure—Asta
will never give up!

Yūki Tabata was born in Fukuoka Prefecture
and got his big break in the 2011 Shonen
Jump Golden Future Cup with his winning
entry, Hungry Joker. He started the magical
fantasy series Black Clover in 2015
